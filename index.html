Who We Are?

This college is situated 1.5km away from kishni on kusmara road. This institution was made by MR. DHARMENDRA YADAV in the memory of his father MR. MADHAV SINGH YADAV in 2008. The main motive for opening this college was to provide quality education to the small village students with completely working labs. To over come all the difficulties faced by village students and after seeing their high interest in studying this institution was build with the name of CHOUDRY MADHAV SINGH SAMARAK MAHAVIDHALAYA. In the first year only we got Affiliated by DR BHIMRAO AMBEDKAR UNIVERSITY,AGRA.

Programs We have

Our programs offer students a platform for academic excellence with subjects that are constructed to provide equal emphasis on theoretical and practical exposure.

Bachelor of Science

A Bachelor of Science Degree, most often abbreviated as a BS or BSc Degree, is an undergraduate science-focused degree that takes 3-5 years to complete. This timeline varies based on the BSC degree area of focus, student needs, and any previously earned transfer credits.

Bachelor of Arts

A Bachelor of Arts is a broad interdisciplinary undergraduate degree program encompassing general education, electives and major area of study courses.By taking courses in the arts and humanities, you can deepen your cultural understanding and awareness, in addition to developing analytical, and communication skills.

Bachelor of Education

Bachelor of Education is a 2 year undergraduate teaching program that enables you to pursue a career in teaching and other related fields. This course can also be commonly known as Bachelor of Training or Shiksha Shastri.Bachelor of education was introduced to train up aspiring teachers in all aspects of teaching.

D.EI.ED

D.El.Ed is a Diploma course in Elementary Education. It is a 2 year full time diploma course to train teachers for the primary level and can be done with classroom training or distance learning. It is a 4 semester course to prepare students to work in primary/ upper primary schools in different states of the country..
